Retinophathy

Retinopathy is any damage to the retina of the eyes, which may cause vision impairment.Retinopathy often refers to retinal vascular disease, or damage to the retina caused by abnormal blood flow

Training and testing set

Split

  • The data was divided in training and testing sets.

  • Dataset Split is made on a patient-level instead of image-level.

    • Each patient has two images: left and right.
    • Both images are not independent.
    • Train/Testing split dataset was done by patients.

    80% patients were in training & 20% of the patients were assigned to testing set.

Models

Arquitectures

  • Tested Architectures: Resnet50, EfficientNetB0, EfficientNetB3.

  • Transfer learning was used as baseline with weigths from “imagenet”

  • Image size tested as inputs:

    • 320x320x3
    • 224x2243
    • 150x150x3

Training details:

  • Tensorflow & Keras.
  • Adam Optimizer.
  • Learning rate: 1x10-4
  • Loss: Weighted Kappa Loss with Cuadratic weigths.
  • Evaluation metric: Weithed Accuracy.
  • Epochs: 100
  • batch_size: 64
